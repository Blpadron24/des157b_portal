Des 157b

Capstone Projects

Welcome to the showcase of student projects for the Davis Centennial Seal. The seal, created by Susan Shelton, celebrates the 100th anniversary of the 1917 incorporation of the city of Davis. It is located at 604 Second St in downtown Davis.

The DES 157B (Interactive Media III) courses are taught by glenda drew and Bill Mead. Learn more about the class here.

Designed and developed by Brenda Padron
